Archy

Archy is my second OC, a very small Dovabrodian with a very big heart.

Basic details

Archy's age is unknown, measuring only 15cm tall (head to feet), 45cm long (nose to tail), and a wingspan of 60cm.

Archy features golden scales with white underbelly scales, blue eyes, and bright orange tipped feathers along his body, tail, and wings.

Archy has the ability to change his size, with the downside that the process consumes a lot of energy. Due to the amount of energy used he prefers being his usual size, using the ability only when the situation calls for it.
